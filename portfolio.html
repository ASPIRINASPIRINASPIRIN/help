COLOR ME! [Poster/2018]

The poster I designed with the drawings and words from one day workshop with KABK students. With the given albino animal pictures, they had to color and add new characteristics for them. After that, they had to choose the adjectives which can describe those.

THE LIFE OF MARGINALITY [Wall newspaper/2019]

Wall newspaper design which inspired by "Choosing the Margin as a Space of Radical Openness" by Bell Hooks. Sublimation-printed on textile.

WE LIVE IN A RAINBOW OF CHAOS [Album cover design/2020]

Mailing art/Album design project which is contributed by KABK students in pandemic. Inspired from the mail arts of "Correspondence School", and the quote of Paul Cezzane. "We live in a rainbow of chaos." - Paul Cezzane

ANTARTIC PENGUINS [Book/2020]

Redesigned the penguin observation logbook which is almost a hundred years old. Original book is "Antartic Penguins" by Dr.G.Murray Levick R.N. Used Operetta and Times New Roman. Printed and binded by Taesan Indigo in South Korea.

FRAGMENTS OF OBSCURITY [Video/2020]

A self-initiated project which is based on the drawings of old memories. "The idea for this project starts from a school where I had spent ten years of my childhood.
The memories of what I've been through at this school are quite old, faded, and even fragmented.
Hence, when I looked at the diary which written 10 years ago, it was full of episodes
that I couldn't remember at all, so felt like it is not that realistic like a dream.
Then I put these fragmented memories together, which are as if I had only experienced in vision."

LIKE THE COSMOS IN THE DIRT [Video/Book/2020]

Video and visual book which inspired by Donna Haraway's "Compost-ist" term. "Life on Earth is such a good story you cannot afford to miss the beginning...
Beneath our superficial differences we are all of us walking communities of bacteria.
The world shimmers, a pointillist landscape made of tiny living beings" - Lynn Margulis

ASTRO REGULAR [Font Design/2020]

Modular-type typeface design inspired by the form of a galaxy.
Only available for lower case.

NO ONE IS TOO SMALL TO MAKE A DIFFERENCE [BOOK/2020]

Book re-designing project which is mostly focused on the tone from the speeches of Greta Thunberg. Used symbolic, and representative images of global warming, instead of re-using the words itself. Original book is "No One is Too Small to Make a Difference" by Greta Thunberg. Used PT Serif and Nepulus. Printed and binded in Den Haag.
